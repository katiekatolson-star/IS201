Five French Idioms You Should Know

  1. "Avoir la pêche"
    • "to have the peach"
    • used to express feeling great or energetic
  2. "Faire la grasse matinée"
    • "to do the fat morning"
    • used to express sleeping in
  3. "Raconter des salades"
    • "to tell salads"
    • used to express telling lies or nonsense
  4. "Avoir un coup de foudre"
    • "to be struck by lightning"
    • used to express love at first sight
  5. "En faire tout un fromage"
    • "to make a whole cheese out of it"
    • used to express making a big deal out of something
